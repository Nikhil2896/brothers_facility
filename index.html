Company History

Back in October 2020, Brothers Facility Services was born out of a simple idea: to provide top-notch cleaning services that truly made a difference. We saw a need for a company that not only cleaned spaces but created healthy and welcoming environments. From the very beginning, we focused on attention to detail, reliability, and building strong relationships with our clients. It's been an incredible journey so far, and we're proud of the growth we've achieved while staying true to our core values of hard work, integrity, and a passion for creating a cleaner, healthier NSW.

Operations

At Brothers Facility Services, we're dedicated to providing a wide range of cleaning and maintenance solutions throughout New South Wales, with a particular focus on the Illawarra and Sydney regions. Our operational focus lies in delivering high-quality, reliable services to a diverse clientele, including commercial businesses, residential properties, and public spaces. We strive to maintain the highest standards of cleanliness and professionalism in every project we undertake.

Company Values

At Brothers Facility Services, we value integrity, customer satisfaction, and teamwork. We operate with honesty and transparency, building strong client relationships. Our team thrives in a collaborative environment, and we continuously innovate to deliver the most effective cleaning solutions.
